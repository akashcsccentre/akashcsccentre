Our Story

Bringing Government Services Closer to You

Akash CSC Centre is a government-authorised Common Service Centre (CSC) located in Aimnabad, Sector-1, Greater Noida West. We are committed to bridging the gap between citizens and digital government services — making them accessible, reliable, and transparent for everyone in our community.

As a certified Village Level Entrepreneur (VLE) under the CSC scheme of MeitY, we serve residents across Greater Noida West, helping them access critical government schemes without travelling to distant offices.

Help & FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common queries about our services, timings, and how we can help you.

What is a CSC Centre (Common Service Centre)?+

A Common Service Centre (CSC) is a government-authorised digital service delivery point under MeitY, providing citizens access to government e-services, banking, insurance and digital services in one place. Akash CSC Centre is an officially certified CSC point serving Greater Noida West.

What are your working hours?+

We are open Monday to Saturday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. On Sundays we are open from 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM for urgent services. You can also WhatsApp us at +91 7209825995 for any enquiries.

How do I apply for Ayushman Bharat (PMJAY) card?+

Bring your Aadhaar card and ration card (or other eligible documents). We check your eligibility on the official PM-JAY portal and, if eligible, process your registration and generate your health card. There is no government fee for registration — you pay only the nominal CSC service charge.

Can I withdraw or deposit cash here for my SBI account?+

Yes! As a certified SBI Business Correspondent, we offer cash deposit, withdrawal, balance enquiry, mini statements, and AEPS fund transfers for SBI account holders. Please bring your Aadhaar card and registered mobile number.

What documents do I need to open a bank account?+

For opening an SBI savings or Jan Dhan account: Aadhaar card (mandatory), PAN card or Form 60, one passport-size photograph, and your mobile number. We guide you through the entire process.

How can I apply for a loan through your centre?+

We process loans through CSC Loan Bazaar, connecting borrowers with banks and NBFCs. You can apply for personal, home, business, and Kisan Credit Card loans. Common documents needed: Aadhaar, PAN, income proof, and bank statements.

What insurance policies can I get here?+

As a certified insurance agent who cleared the RAP exam from CSC, we offer life insurance, health insurance, general insurance (vehicle, travel), and PMFBY crop insurance for farmers. We help you compare and choose the right plan for your needs.

How long does Aadhaar or PAN card take?+

Aadhaar updates are processed at the centre immediately — acknowledgement slip given on the spot, updated Aadhaar available within 7–15 working days from UIDAI. PAN card applications are submitted to NSDL/UTI and delivered within 7–15 working days. An e-PAN can be downloaded sooner.

Are your service charges the official government rates?+

Yes. All government services have prescribed fee structures set by the respective departments. We charge only the official service fee plus the regulated CSC service charge. No hidden fees. Verify anytime on the official CSC Digital Seva portal.

How do I reach your centre?+

We are at Shop No.3, Plot No.2, KHS-782, Dhyan Singh Bhati Road, Aimnabad, Sector-1, Greater Noida West, UP – 201318. Call or WhatsApp us on +91 7209825995 for directions. See the map on our Contact page.
